These paintings of porn scenarios are painted over photographs culled from online porn sites. The photographs are from the 1970s and 1980s, a period known as "The Golden Age of Porn". Using paint, I try to mimic as closely as possible the underlying photograph. The gesture itself is almost imperceptible - just enough to transform the image.
